# Garuda-Hacks
JALIN is an AI-powered B2B Business Intelligence & Matching Platform that helps food producers and buyers discover, evaluate, and connect with the most suitable business partners across Indonesia. It acts as a business networking and intelligence platform where producers create verified company profiles showcasing their products, production capacity, certifications, minimum order quantity (MOQ), and business information, while buyers publish their sourcing needs and business requirements. Using AI-powered compatibility matching, JALIN analyzes both producer and buyer profiles to recommend the best business matches based on multiple factors, helping users find qualified partners faster and with greater confidence. To build trust, businesses can verify their identity through Indonesia's NIB (Business Identification Number), while integrated negotiation workspaces and agreement forms allow both parties to discuss opportunities before revealing contact information. By replacing manual networking and word-of-mouth referrals with intelligent business matching, JALIN reduces partner discovery time, expands market access for MSMEs, minimizes missed business opportunities, and contributes to a more efficient and connected food supply chain. 

The platform is built using React + Vite for the frontend, Go (Gin) for the backend, PostgreSQL with GORM for database management, JWT and RBAC for secure authentication and authorization, and AI-powered recommendation algorithms that continuously improve business matching accuracy.
